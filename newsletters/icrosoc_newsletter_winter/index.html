Ice Skating @
Natural History Museum

Join us for one of our most popular events, this coming Monday, November 23rd, at 19:00!
Members will have a discount of £5, and the event will also include a drink - a nice warm cup of hot chocolate, or mulled wine. Tickets are available for purchase from the Union shop website, here. However, due to the popularity of this event, they are available to IC RoSoc members only. So, you can still grab your membership from here and join us for lots of fun!


 

National Day Celebration Party @
Cornelius Restaurant

Before putting on your Christmas jumpers, let's not forget about our National Day! Like every year, Romanian students from London are gathering together at a Romanian restaurant in order to celebrate our beloved country. There will be Romanian food and music, and lots of people to mingle and have fun with! The event will take place on December 1st, at 19:30, at Cornelius restaurant (Heron House , Ferry Lane, N17 9NF, London). Tickets can be bought from Corina, by dropping her an email, or a message on Facebook). More information on the event can be found here. Hope you can make it!


 

Winter Wonderland for
St. Nick's Celebration

Every year, Hyde Park becomes a magical place, filled with candy, elfs, polar bears and ice. Some say it's the Christmas Spirit transforming the park, others that the actual park is an illusion meant to hide the North Pole. Whatever the answer is, we like it and we think you will too. So join us, whether you want to explore Santa's shop, the elfs' Christmas market or simply enjoy the rides. Date remains to be announced, but we're aiming for St. Nick's celebration (5-6th of December).
